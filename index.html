Jake was raised in Pickton, Texas, and after round one of college was a Technical Theatre director for six years across three of the largest school districts in Texas. When they got sick and had to involuntarily retire to remote work, Jonathan's love, care, encouragement, and compassion while they navigated one of the most difficult times in their life made Jake fall quickly. After several months they moved in with Jonathan and trained Luca while navigating several moves together. Jake ordered a ring (to reverse‐propose when Jonathan was ready) the week before Jonathan was diagnosed, and once the couple got engaged Jake went back to school to support their new family with their new needs. Jake is excited to join Jonathan spiritually before the couple moves and begins their new adventure that they've both been working toward for over two years.
